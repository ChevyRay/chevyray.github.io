Skullpogo

Skullpogo is a mobile game I created with Justin Smith (Desert Golfing, Enviro-Bear 2010).

By tilting your phone, you can bounce your character around on a pogo stick. Your goal is to bounce on enemies as fast as possible in order to chain together combos. Various powerups and special enemy occurences give you the opportunity to vastly extend your combos.
