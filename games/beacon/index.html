Beacon

Beacon is a dark platformer I made for Ludum Dare 15.

You find yourself in a dark cavern, cold and naked, unable to see in the pitch black. A shining beacon appears to lead you to safety, so you follow it.

Stay in the beacon's light, lest you rot in the darkness.
