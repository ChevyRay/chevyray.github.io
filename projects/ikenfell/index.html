Ikenfell

My current game project! It's by far my most ambitious one, too.

Ikenfell is a turn-based RPG about witches, wizards, the school they attend, & the trouble they get into. It is also a game about love, friendships, rivalries, mischief... and kissing. The kissing may or may not happen between any of the former.

Ikenfell was successfully funded on Kickstarter!
